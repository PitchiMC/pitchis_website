A clean, performance-oriented resource pack designed for competitive play. Clarity meets aesthetics.

Download on Modrinth Support Discord

Competitive Edge

WhiteNight PvP is built for players who need maximum visibility without sacrificing style. Every texture is optimized to reduce visual clutter during intense fights.


From shortened swords to clear GUI elements, this pack ensures that your focus remains on your opponent. It's the perfect companion for BedWars, SkyWars, or The Bridge

Pack Features

  • FPS Boost: Optimized textures for better performance.
  • Low Fire: Reduced fire height for better visibility.
  • Short Swords: Sleek weapon models for a better view.
  • Clean GUI: Transparent and modern interface design.
